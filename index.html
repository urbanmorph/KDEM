Karnataka's Competitive Position

National Leadership

  • #1 in IT Exports (44% of India)
  • #1 in GCCs (550+ centers, 35-40% of India)
  • #1 in Unicorns (45+, representing 50% of India)
  • #1 in DPIIT Startup Rankings (Consistently)
  • #1 in VC Activity (90% concentration in Bengaluru)
  • World #2 AI/ML Talent Hub (600,000 professionals)

Competitive Alert: Telangana

Challenge: Telangana attracted 70 greenfield GCCs in 2024-25 vs. Karnataka's 40+

Cost Advantage: Hyderabad office rents ₹72/sq ft vs. Bengaluru's ₹95/sq ft

Salary Differential: 15-25% lower than Bengaluru for similar roles

KDEM Strategic Response:

  • Accelerating Beyond Bengaluru GCC strategy
  • Targeting 100 mid-sized GCCs in cost-competitive Tier-2 locations
  • Mysuru, Mangaluru, Hubballi positioned as affordable alternatives
  • GCC Policy 2024: 500 new GCCs by 2029

Data Sources & Methodology

Attribution Transparency Framework

KDEM operates as a facilitator and enabler rather than a direct operator. We employ a three-tier attribution model:

Level A: Direct Impact

ELEVATE-funded startups, documented facilitation cases, program beneficiaries

High Confidence

Level B: Influenced Outcomes

Event conversions, policy advocacy outcomes, facilitated connections

Medium Confidence

Level C: Ecosystem Health

Overall Beyond Bengaluru growth, Karnataka rankings, state-wide employment

Key Assumptions & Projections

  • Digital Economy Growth: Assumes compound annual growth rate of 25-30% to reach $400B by 2030 from current ~$110B (2025 baseline)
  • Beyond Bengaluru Attribution: Companies and jobs are counted as "onboarded" when KDEM has documented facilitation role through letters, event connections, or program participation
  • GCC Growth Trajectory: 500 new GCCs target assumes 100 GCCs/year average from 2025-2029, requiring acceleration from current ~40/year
  • Cluster Targets: Mysuru $10B (2030) and Mangaluru ₹40,000 Cr (2034) based on official Vision Documents released by KDEM
  • Competitive Data: Telangana GCC data from Zinnov reports, NASSCOM analysis, and state government announcements; validated through multiple sources
  • Employment Figures: Job creation numbers based on company self-reporting to KDEM, cross-validated with EPFO/ESI data where available
  • Startup Metrics: DPIIT registration data used as primary source; funding data from Tracxn/Inc42 validated quarterly
  • Update Frequency: Dashboard data reflects latest available information as of January 2026; quarterly updates planned
